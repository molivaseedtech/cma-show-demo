Our story

From radio mornings to a daily digital show.

Carla Marie and Anthony met in 2009 working for Elvis Duran and The Morning Show. Five years later they launched a weekly podcast, MyDayFriday, just to get better on air. It paid off — they landed their own radio morning show in Seattle in 2016 and road-tripped from New Jersey to Washington to build The Carla Marie and Anthony Show.

After building a loyal community on the air, they were let go from their radio jobs in 2020. Two days later they took everything digital and built their own business. Today they produce the show live on YouTube and Twitch and release The Morning Show Podcast every weekday — always under 25 minutes.

They've also launched their own apparel line built around their favorite saying, "You Look Great!" And somewhere along the way, they started dating. What they're proudest of is the community.
